About Us

Tanu and her cooking crew of sisters, strictly select fresh ingredients on a daily basis from the local farmer market. Conscious cooking is applied to every dish prepared in Tanu’s Kitchen.

We are not a restaurant. We are a proud Indian kitchen run by empowered independent British Asian women. Our mission is to feed our guests with fresh (made from scratch) food that reminds them of Mum’s cocking at home.

Our simplified pricing, makes it easier for you to order.




OUR SPECIALITY






DELHI CHAAT
Delhi Chaat is famous north Indian street food.
INDO CHINESE
Indo-Chinese food takes Chinese culinary styles and adds spices and flavors familiar to the Indian tongue.
FRESH PARANTHA
Parathas are one of the most popular unleavened flatbreads in India, made by whole wheat dough on a tawa, and finishing off with shallow frying.
